Tutorial

Welcome to Dendron

This guide will help you learn the basics of Dendron. You can also follow the Getting Started guides on our website.

By the end of the tutorial, you will also see how Dendron can help you manage your knowledge with super fast lookup, flexible hierarchies, and powerful linking.

If you close VS Code, you can always get back to this workspace by going to File > Open Workspace in VS Code's menu and then selecting your workspace file: C:\Users\kiwij\Dendron\dendron.code-workspace

The tutorial steps take about 10 minutes to complete. Let's get started!

Steps to get started with Dendron:

  • Install Dendron
  • Create your first workspace
  • Complete the Tutorial (Ctrl+Click on the links to go to each tutorial note)
    • One: Navigation Basics
    • Two: Taking Notes
    • Three: Linking Your Notes
    • Four: Rich Formatting
    • Five: Conclusion

Getting Help

  • If you get stuck, take a look at our full documentation on our wiki. Don't hesitate to reach out to our community of Dendrologists on Discord or message us on Twitter at @dendronhq
