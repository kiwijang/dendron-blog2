Commutative Laws

(x AND y) = (y AND x)
(x OR y) = (y OR x)

Associative Laws

(x AND (y AND z)) = ((x AND y) AND z)) (x OR (y OR z)) = ((x OR y) OR z))

Distributive Laws

(x AND (y OR z)) = (x AND y) OR (x AND z) (x OR (y AND z)) = (x OR y) AND (x OR z)

De Morgan Laws

NOT(x AND y) = NOT(x) OR NOT(y) NOT(x OR y) = NOT(x) AND NOT(y)
